Welcome to PulseVitals

Before we start measuring, here's what PulseVitals does with your data:

  • Measures only the active tab's performance data.
  • Stores recent-run history on your device in browser storage.

4.1 Privacy policy (privacy.html)
file://…/privacy.html or https://pulsevitals.app/privacy

Privacy policy

Last updated: 2026-04-17

What we collect

PulseVitals measures page-load performance metrics (LCP, CLS, INP, nav-timing, resources, storage size) from tabs you visit. It runs only on the active tab while the extension is installed.

What we store

Measurement snapshots are stored locally in chrome.storage.local, capped at 10 snapshots per origin. Options are stored in chrome.storage.sync and roam across your Chrome profile.

What we don't do

We don't send your data to any server. No telemetry endpoint, no crash reporting, no analytics. Everything stays on your device.

Your controls

Clear all history from the Options page. Uninstalling the extension deletes all stored data.
