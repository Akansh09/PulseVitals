Measurement

Auto-measure on every page load
Captures CWV + timings silently; popup reads cached data. Off means the popup shows "measuring…" until you interact.
Mobile-default emulation
Always re-run reports under a mobile viewport (375 px) + CPU throttle. Approximate — content scripts can't truly throttle.
Show platform-aware tips
Detect Shopify / WordPress / Webflow / Next.js and append platform-specific fix recipes in the Insights section.
Show SEO checks
Adds a Popup section with on-page SEO heuristics (title, meta, H1, canonical, OG, viewport, JSON-LD).
Show accessibility score
Runs a bundled 8-rule axe-lite check and shows a combined 0–100 Health Score.
Pin overlay on page
Floating CWV badge in the bottom-right of every page (Shadow-DOM, doesn't affect page styles). Dismissible per-tab with ×.
